Brief Biography

Prof. Sergey Valentinovich Sushkov is Deputy Director for Research at the V.T. Zaitsev Institute of General and Emergency Surgery (NAMS Ukraine) and Professor at the Department of Surgery №1, Kharkiv National Medical University. His clinical and research career spans nearly 40 years across general, oncological, vascular and thoracic surgery.

He is one of the pioneers of laparoscopic surgery in the Kharkiv region (1995–1997) and has maintained the highest qualification category in five surgical specialties. Over the last two decades he has combined operative practice with translational research, clinical trials management and medical education at the residency and fellowship level.

Current research interests include surgical outcomes in emergency and oncological abdominal surgery, minimally invasive techniques, and AI-assisted decision support in surgery.
